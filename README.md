# 🚀 Superstore Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
The goal of this project is to analyze the sales data of a Superstore to understand business performance, identify profitability trends, and highlight areas for improvement. I transformed raw transactional data into an interactive, dynamic dashboard to help stakeholders make data-driven decisions regarding product performance and regional sales strategies.

## 🎯 Business Objectives
* Identify the highest revenue-generating and most profitable products.
* Analyze geographical performance to find the best and worst-performing regions.
* Track monthly sales trends to understand seasonality.
* Evaluate the overall profit margin and health of the business.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
* **Data Visualization & Analytics:** Tableau
* **Data Modeling & Aggregation:** SQL
* **Data Cleaning & Exploration:** Excel

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
At a glance, the business generated:
* **Total Sales:** ₹ 2.30M
* **Total Profit:** ₹ 286.40K
* **Total Orders:** 10K
* **Profit Margin:** 12.47%

## 💡 Key Business Insights
1. **Technology Drives Profitability:** The *Technology* category is the backbone of the store's profitability, contributing over 50% of the total profit (₹145.4K), whereas *Furniture* is underperforming at just 6.4%.
2. **Hero Products:** The *Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier* is the most successful product by a wide margin, generating ₹61.5K in revenue and ₹25.1K in sheer profit.
3. **Regional Dominance:** The *West* region is the strongest market, driving ₹725K in total sales. 
4. **Profit Leaks:** While overall sales are healthy, there are high-revenue items (like the *Cisco TelePresence System*) that are actually resulting in negative profit margins. These pricing or supply chain strategies need immediate review.

## 📂 Project Structure
* `Dataset/`: Contains the raw `Superstore.csv` data.
* `SQL Queries/`: Contains the `.sql` files used to extract top-selling products and regional aggregates.
* `Dashboard/`: Contains the `.twb` (Tableau) workbook.
* `Screenshots/`: Visuals of the dashboard for quick viewing.

## 🚀 How to Run the Project
1. Clone this repository to your local machine.
2. Open the `Superstore.csv` file to view the raw data.
3. Run the SQL scripts in your preferred SQL environment to see the data transformation steps.
4. Open the Tableau workbook (`.twb`) to interact with the dashboard.
